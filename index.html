Peer Feedback Insights

Positive
Peers enjoyed the layout and information in the slide deck
Many people thought the idea would be potentially beneficial to them, as it would save time when grocery shopping or provide directions in unfamiliar stores
The map visuals were detailed and it was easy to see the route provided through the store
The scope of the product was reasonable and could be used by a large domain of shoppers

Negative

Audience for product may not be broad enough
May not be useful to customers already familiar with the store
Malicious users could potentially skew data within the feedback section of the app
Prototype could have been more developed

Peer Feedback Reflections

Knowledge Gained from Peer Feedback
Overall our feedback was mainly positive, with users stating they would use the app and that it is a great solution to the problem of finding items in a grocery store. Our feedback on accessibility was also positive, and in the future, we should mainly focus on allowing the customization of visual elements such as font size and dark mode. Negative feedback mainly focused on the UI sometimes feeling cluttered and the app's reliance on accurate user feedback.
We also noticed most of our users stated they were unlikely to use the audio features to guide them through the store, making this an area we could look to improve by finding ways to integrate audio in less intrusive ways.

Future Work

Potential Upcoming Developments
A few users provided valuable suggestions for future improvements to add and some potential pitfalls of the products, which would be key areas to focus on in later releases of our product. One of the main areas to work on would be adding support for this app to as many stores as possible. We could also work with stores on a case-by-case basis to see if they would be willing to provide coupons or discounts to users who provide feedback and make the app more accurate. We also could look to implement safeguards against users submitting incorrect information. This could be done by giving each user a feedback accuracy rating that shifts on whether future customers find their information to be accurate, and time out users from providing feedback when their rating gets too low. For our product to be successful, we would need to continue to improve the functionality, accuracy, and availability of the app.
